The reading record of a paper includes the following 5 parts.

<hr>

## background

The *background* is about the following aspects.

- **what field** does the paper focus on?
- what's the **basic knowledge** in this field for this work? 

<hr>

## motivation

The *motivation* is about the following aspects. Sometimes it can be combined with the *background*.

- what are the **key problems** want to solve in this work?
- what are some **interesting observations** they find?

<hr>

## design

The *Design* is about the following aspects.

-  what is the **key insight** they hold according to the observation?
- what are the **novel methods** they design to solve the problems? 

<hr>

## evaluation

The *Evaluation* is about the following aspects.

- What are the **advantages** of the methods shown in all-around evaluation?
- what are the **limitations** of the methods shown in all-around evaluation?


<hr>

## conclusion

The *Conclusion* is about the following aspects.

- paper summary

  - key problem and their observation

  - the novel methods

  - the advantages and limitations

    > Not only about the methods but also about **the paper writing**.

- your **further thinking**

  - what can you learn?
  - how can you improve it?
  - what's your thinking according to the background and similar field
